What We Do

We help brands create magnetism with consumers, business audiences and employees through a wide range of effective live and digital experiences that stimulate the target and motivate response.

Utilizing targeted insights, strategic creativity, and armed with an exceptional team, we carefully tailor our programs to provide entertainment and value that is relevant and compelling, and we do it right across Canada.

The Agency

At Magnet Engagement Group, we ensure brands gain ground and succeed in the market through strategic, integrated live + digital experiences. We offer clients the creative solutions, expertise, and resources to influence consumer buying behavior and deliver results.

Our Process

Through meaningful and personal interactions, we create a foundation that leads to advocacy for our clients’ brands. For every campaign, we build consumer loyalty for brands through an immersive experience. Then, we amplify it and deliver results.

From integrated, multi-year campaigns to grassroots, one-off activations, Magnet responds to our clients’ changing needs to succeed in a fast-paced marketplace, and the programs that we create with our clients captivate and sustain consumer interest by leveraging passion points.

We listen to what your consumers, your retail partners, and your employees are saying. From that, we create and deliver an engaging, relevant story. We truly connect.
